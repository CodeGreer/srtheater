Help Wanted

There are many ways you can volunteer for Staged Right Theatre!

All of our shows will need house and crew help! If you are interested in working in the box office, backstage, or with sets, props or costumes, contact the director of each show or send an email to our general email address: stagedrighttheatrega@gmail.com

For those interested in long-term involvement, we currently have two committees you can join:



Fundraising Committee

Committee Head: Kendal Franklin, Managing Director

Description:
As a member of Staged Right’s fundraising committee, you will be part of a creative, dynamic group involved in planning engaging, exciting fundraisers throughout the year, under the guidance of our Managing Director.

The committee will help organize the events, setting budgets, recruiting volunteers and sponsors, and implementing a marketing plan.

During the event, the committee members will be hands on, making sure the event runs smoothly, money is secured, and patrons have a phenomenal time!

Being a member of the fundraising committee affords you the opportunity to flex your creativity, work with a great group of people, and be an essential part of Staged Right Theatre!

Responsibilities:
Meetings as needed
Planning and working events

Time commitment:
1 year commitment, renewable in November of each year

If interested, contact Kendal at omunroe13@yahoo.com



Play Selection Committee

Committee Head: Starshine Stanfield, Artistic Director

Description:
As a member of Staged Right’s play selection committee, you will have the opportunity to read and recommend plays for the next season, under the guidance of the Artistic Director.

With a set theme in mind, committee members will research potential scripts, and present recommendations at monthly meetings.

As the play selection is narrowed down, committee members will participate in group readings of the plays, to assist the Artistic Director in narrowing the choices.

Being a member of the play selection committee affords you the opportunity to read, discuss, and analyze plays, and be an essential part of Staged Right Theatre’s season!

Responsibilities:
Monthly meetings
Reading 2-3 plays a month

Time commitment:
1 year commitment, renewable in April of each year

If interested, contact Starshine at starshine@starshinesjourney.com
